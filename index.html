Heritage & Experience

A Jaipur Dining Landmark With Royal Soul

Peacock Restaurant is known for its rooftop charm, regal mood, and memorable cuisine in Jaipur. Inspired by the city’s royal culture, the experience blends rich ambience, elevated service, and beautifully plated food in a setting built for special evenings.

The Peacock Story

Where Heritage Dining Meets Modern Luxury

From travellers and food lovers to couples and celebrations, Peacock has earned its place as one of Jaipur’s most visually memorable dining destinations. The mood is elegant, atmospheric, and deeply rooted in the city’s royal identity.

Every visit is designed to feel special — from the lighting and rooftop setting to the menu presentation and the richness of the flavours. The result is more than dinner. It becomes an event.
